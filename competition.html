Accepted Versions: Super BTW or BTW CE 2.1.4. You may use Ears, Neodymium, Random Things, and Dynamic Lighting addons. I guess you can do it on MEA too...

1. Your run is complete when you pull the diamond 💎💎💎 pickaxe off the crafting table.

2. 🌎 You must start from a fresh world. Dying and respawning is okay.

3. You must use a random seed during world creation. 🌱

4. Survival mode only. No using creative mode, no teleporting, no giving yourself items, no looking up coordinates or map information in any way. 💀 Cheating is for the weak.

5. You must record 📹 your run and post it online! Record world creation.
